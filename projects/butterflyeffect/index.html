Why BE?


We knew and believed education could be much more than it is was in Ghana. Education should be more than memorising and regurgitating information in Mathematics, Science and English. It should be about using your knowledge to create transformational change. Education should prepare you for life and not merely examinations. In short, we believe education should be a tool for you to unearth your God-giving potential to create change in your life and in your community. Armed with this belief, we reflected on how we were going to transform education in Ghana into what it should be. From this reflection birthed the praxis of Butterfly Effect.

The name Butterfly Effect was derived from the theory of the same name, which states that if you change even the smallest of life's details, you completely change its outcome. Inspired by this, Butterfly Effect is an educational enterprise that creates educational experiences for individuals, especially youth to realize their ability to develop themselves and their communities. We are confident these revolutionary experiences will act as a conduit for the generation of think-out-the-box trendsetters who have the skills, sense of self, and spark to improve their life outcomes and reshape Ghanaian society.

What Do We Do?


We create revolutionary educational experiences through which young people become changemakers ready to take on their worlds. At the moment we have two of such experiences existing and others under construction.

Public School Curriculum

The Public School Curriculum is designed to promote critical thinking, creativity, communication, teamwork and independent learning that moves beyond rote memorisation typical of a traditional curriculum.[Read More]

Financial Literacy Curriculum

Through a series of exciting, age appropriate and localised activities. this one day curriculum has been designed to equip students with the tools to learn the basics of managing money.

Join Us

Butterfly Effect is a multiple award and Scholarship winning enterprise transforming the educational system to harness the full potential of each child through experiences rather than theory based curricula. We are currently recruiting personnel for the improvement of our Public School Curriculum.

We aim to make our curriculum easy for schools to implement and for this reason we have created a network of personnel who have committed to be called upon to play a variety of roles listed below This way, at every point in the curriculum that requires personnel, partner schools can have a wide range of people to contact. Parts of our curriculum require personnel to:

- Deliver skill workshops
- Share experiences
- Provide expert feedback

If you are an individual with experience in any Career field or possess a particular skill that can be taught or have experience in starting up a business, please reach out to us and join our network.

Volunteer

If you cannot make a long term commitment but are still interested in what we do, there are many opportunities and avenues through which you can contribute. E-mail us at butterflyeffectghana@gmail.com and tell us more about how you would like to get involved.


Partner Institutions

If you are a school or any other youth-focused institution and would like to adopt one of our existing curricula or have a bespoke one created, please contact us at butterflyeffectghana@gmail.com. We would love to work with you.

Mentor

If you are looking for an internship opportunity or a fulfilling commitment, apply to be a mentor with Butterfly Effect! We have already recruited for this Academic cycle but if you are still interested in getting involved as a mentor, e-mail us at butterflyeffectghana@gmail.com and we will reach out to you in the next recruitment cycle. Mentors are required (after training) to

-Deliver B.E. Curriculum twice a week to up to 70 students in Partner schools
- Empower and guide students as they implement their initiatives

Requirements include passion for the cause, 1 Year commitment and previous related experience
